Welcome!

This is a simple game of Rock-Paper-Scissors (aka "Ro-Sham-Bo"; janken; "Bato, Bato, Pick"; and "Scissors, Paper, Stone")

The rules:

You beat another player if your choice "beats" theirs in the following way:

Rock beats Scissors

Scissors beat Paper

Paper beats Rock

Each round you score 1 for other player you beat. Player score will be displayed after a round on the bottom of your console.

Ending and winning the game:

At the end of round press 'y' to continue.
Any other key to exit the game.

You decide when you want to finish the game.


In case of a Tie:

None of the players get score. The draw counter will increase by 1.

Have fun!
